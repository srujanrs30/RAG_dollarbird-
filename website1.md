## Our Story
A Journey from ordinary to extraordinary!
### All it took was one determined thought!
We all know that when a person is in a low state, they either tend to give up or decide to become an influencer. The Founder of Dollarbird, with all his might and struggles, chose to be an influencer, and that's where the story starts.
After a lot of struggle, in 2010, he decided to start a new journey, but with a different approach, While there were a lot of people-oriented approaches going on in service-providing firms, he decided to stick with a process-oriented approach. As his career began in digital advertising, he decided to dwell in that area of serving.
## Why the name Dollarbird?
How interesting is it to see the beautiful colors of the actual bird with the name Dollarbird? In the same way, we wanted to grow in such a manner that we also made our clients' lives colorful, we decided to fly to different levels along with our clients, hence the name DOLLARBIRD.
### How did it all start?
It all started with two employees, one client, and one service, always keeping the vision alive in the work we offered, and that has transformed Dollarbird into a company that has 250+ employees, a huge client base, and 40+ services. It all started with a different approach that lighted up the whole world of process-oriented services. That's where we make a difference. As much as our clients are important, our employees matter the most to us, What would we do without them? We could not have made it this far without the trust and support of our clients and employees. In all this, we never let our vision die in us.
### Where we are now?
With a 200% increase in client conversions and a 400% increase in jobs, Dollarbird has seen massive growth over the last five years. We kept our business operating, even during COVID, and were able to employ people who needed jobs in this industry. While Dollarbird's client base was always high in the US, we're expanding into other regions and spreading quickly. Our sales team is growing in countries like the US and Israel, which helps us and our clients grow together.
